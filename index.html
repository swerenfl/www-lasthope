History

The history of <Last Hope> dates back to 2008 when Gluteas started it on Thunderhorn. The guild yearned for mythic content and decided to uproot to Sargeras. Today's <Last Hope> looks a bit different as there are now Orcs and Undead. We still raid but not at the level of yesteryear. We are passionate players who enjoy the game and hope you join us on our journey.

Philosophy

Our philosophy is simple: "raid smarter, not harder". Cutting Edge was hard on our condensed schedule but we did it and we made sure to have fun while doing it. This is a game overall. Because we like to have fun and not stress, <Last Hope>'s primary focus will be Heroic content with a foot in Mythic. Our raiding hours will be: Sunday 9pm - 12am CT.
